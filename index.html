About me

My name is Charlie and I am a self-taught programmer currently living in Manchester. Though I have been working various account manager roles since I left university, I have always been attracted to more technical fields, hence choosing a Nuclear Science degree.


After leaving my previous employed in October 2020, and following a discussion with my brother-in-law who is a software product manager, I decided to support myself while concentrating full time on completing the Full-Stack Programmer course on Codecademy. However, realising that I was particularly enjoying the creative element of CSS and HTML, I was advised that I might be more suited to the Front-End. I therefore completed what I was told were the "necessary" modules of the Full-Stack course for Front-End development.


After beginning to apply for roles in February 2021, I realised that many of the jobs in my area were PHP and SQL focused, so I took another month off to complete the PHP and SQL courses (once again on Codecademy).
